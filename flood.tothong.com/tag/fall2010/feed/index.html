fall2010 – f l o o d http://flood.tothong.com ธาริต Tharit = (thara) water + (rit) power Tue, 06 Dec 2011 15:23:12 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=4.4.2 firm / flood / fall 2011 http://flood.tothong.com/2011/11/17/thailand-flooding-2011/ Thu, 17 Nov 2011 20:56:10 +0000 http://flood.tothong.com/?p=27 Continue reading →]]> This blog is the documentation of Firm Tharit Tothong final project for Major Studio – Interface class in MFA Design and Technology at Parsons the New School For Design in Fall 2011. Since it’s the first time Firm have left his home country for such a long time, a strange relationship between he and the emerging of social networks in everyday life gets to the point that his experience seems to be surreal, especially when the major flooding happens in Central Thailand. Everyone he known in Thailand is facing this catastrophe, but for him it feels like just another narrative happens in the computer screen.

Through this project, Firm trying to express his awkward experience from his own perspective and skill sets he learnt from this semester and synthesize it into an installation – the work that he always wanted to do.
